Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is eligible for an education loan?

Students with confirmed admission in recognized colleges/universities and meeting the bank’s criteria are eligible. We guide you through all requirements.

2. How much loan can I get?

The loan amount depends on tuition, living expenses, and bank-specific schemes. We provide advice on maximizing approved amounts.

3. What documents are required?

Common documents include admission letters, identity proof, academic certificates, income proof, and collateral if needed. Our team helps prepare all of them.

4. How long does approval take?

Approval timelines vary by bank and scheme. We assist in tracking your application for timely processing.

5. Can we get loans for studying abroad?

Yes, we provide complete guidance for both domestic and international education loans, including bank recommendations and document support.
